# Mission: Explain TraceBullet in an Interview/Demo

## Why
The user wants to explain TraceBullet clearly in an interview or live demo, including the product problem, the end-to-end flow, and the technical decisions behind the CLI-first implementation.

## Success looks like
- Give a 2-minute product explanation of TraceBullet without drifting into implementation trivia.
- Walk through the command flow from Sentry Issue ID to Deterministic Report.
- Explain why the project uses deterministic matching before any LLM-generated narrative.
- Answer follow-up questions about local data, Coral sandbox data, tests, and the future dashboard prototype.

## Constraints
- Focus on demo fluency and interview explanation, not deep production hardening.
- Use the current repository as the source of truth.
- Keep explanations tied to observable files, commands, and test cases.

## Out of scope
- Building new TraceBullet features.
- Connecting to private or production company data.
- Teaching every detail of Coral, Sentry, GitHub, or Slack independently.
